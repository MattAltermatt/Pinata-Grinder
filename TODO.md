# Pinata Grinder — Weapon Ideas TODO

## New Weapon Types

### Tesla Coil / Lightning
- **Niche**: Chain damage — hits one target then arcs to nearby squares
- Zaps closest square, bolt chains to adjacent squares (electricity jumping)
- Each hop does slightly less damage (80% of previous)
- Upgrades: chain count (1-8 hops), damage, range, arc speed, charge time
- Visual: crackling blue/white LineRenderer arcs that fork between squares
- Rewards tight clusters — opposite of missile's center-mass strategy

### Freeze Ray / Cryo Beam
- **Niche**: Crowd control / support — slows pinatas, amplifies other weapons
- Continuous beam (like laser) that slows target Pinata's gravity scale and velocity
- Frozen squares turn blue/icy and take 2x damage from other weapons
- After enough exposure, squares shatter (instant kill)
- Upgrades: slow strength, freeze-to-shatter time, beam width, range, extra emitters
- First support weapon — creates strategic combos (freeze + missile = devastating)

### Flak Cannon / Shotgun
- **Niche**: Wide spread, close range burst
- Fires a cone of small pellets in a spread pattern
- Each pellet does low damage but many pellets (8-20)
- Very short range, covers wide area
- Upgrades: pellet count, spread angle (tight to wide), range, damage per pellet, fire rate
- Area denial — great at bottom of field where pinatas are close

### Gravity Well / Tractor Beam
- **Niche**: Utility — repositions pinatas into kill zones
- Creates a pull zone that attracts pinatas toward it (or pushes away)
- No direct damage — forces pinatas into other weapons
- Can hold pinatas in place for lasers/saws to shred
- Upgrades: pull strength, radius, push/pull toggle, damage aura, extra wells
- Only weapon that manipulates physics rather than dealing damage

### Rail Gun / Sniper
- **Niche**: High single-target damage, piercing line
- Long charge-up (visible energy building), fires piercing beam through everything in a line
- Damages every square along beam path
- Upgrades: charge speed, damage, beam width, pierce count, extra barrels
- Rewards positioning — line up stopper for beam through maximum pinatas

### Drone Swarm
- **Niche**: Autonomous area coverage
- Deploys small drones that fly independently around field seeking targets
- Each drone does light contact damage (like tiny untethered saws)
- Drones have limited lifetime, launcher deploys replacements
- Upgrades: drone count, drone speed, drone damage, drone lifetime, deploy rate
- Only weapon not operating from stopper position — passive/roaming playstyle
